What exactly is this app?

SubHub is a powerful application designed to help you take control of your digital subscriptions. From a single dashboard, you can track all your recurring payments, monitor spending, receive alerts before renewal dates, and manage your services effortlessly.

Does it work on every device?

Yes! SubHub is designed to be fully responsive and works seamlessly on any device with a modern web browser, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones. We also have dedicated applications for iOS and Android for the best mobile experience.

Which streaming services can I manage?

You can manage a wide variety of services, including Netflix, Spotify, Disney+, Prime Video, HBO Max, YouTube Premium, and many more. Our platform is constantly expanding to support new and popular subscription services.

Can this app also manage my budget?

Absolutely. Budget management is a core feature of SubHub. You can view your total monthly and yearly subscription costs, categorize your spending, and set custom budgets to receive alerts when you're approaching your limits.
